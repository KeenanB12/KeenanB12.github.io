Why LeBron James is the greatest basketball player of all time

LeBron James is the greatest NBA player of all time because of the way he has changed the game throughout his time in the league. He has been arguably the most dominant player in his time and possibly of all time playing the game.

Timeline of LeBron James life so far

  • December 1984 - LeBron James is born
  • June 2003 - LeBron declares for draft straight out of high school
  • June 2008 - LeBron James wins first MVP
  • July 2010 - Makes decision to move to Miami
  • June 2012 - Wins first ring
  • July 2014 - LeBron moves back to Cleveland
  • June 2016 - Wins first ring with the Cavs
  • July 2018 - Signs with Lakers
  • March 2019 - LeBron passes Micael Jordan 4th on all-time scoring list
  • January 2020 - passes Kobe Bryant 3rd all-time on scoring list
